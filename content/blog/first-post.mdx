Distributed systems fail in surprising ways.

After working with high-scale financial systems, I learned three critical lessons.

## 1 — Failures Are the Default

Assume services will fail.

Design retries, circuit breakers, and idempotent APIs.

## 2 — Observability Is Mandatory

Logs, metrics and traces are essential to understand real failures.

## 3 — Simplicity Wins

The most resilient systems are usually the simplest ones.
